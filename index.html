Exun 2018

Bigger and better than ever before.

Join us in celebrating the spirit of technology. 27-28 October

National for the first time ever.

Exun has been organizing its flagship event for two decades. With Exun 2018, the event becomes the first national technology symposium for and by the students.

Events

Build

Build is an event that requires creative participants from a team to come together and build a solution to one of two prompts. The event will test the creative skills of participants.

SpaceTech

Exun Clan is collaborating with the Delhi Chapter of the prestigious National Space Society (USA) to give students the opportunity to explore the realms of space and it’s concurrence with technology.

Sudocrypt

Sudocrypt, Exun Clan's annual online cryptic hunt, will be held over 48 hours from 00:00:00 on 23 October 2018 to 11:59:59 on 24 October 2018.
